Love Your Spouse Retreat

The beautiful city of Queenstown, New Zealand will be the host city for Love Your Spouse Weekend Retreat!

Explore new and exciting ways of expressing love to your spouse. Discover the ultimate purpose of your marriage in the eyes of God, your Creator.

Retreat Speakers

Take a step back from the busyness of life with a weekend away with your spouse!

Love Your Spouse is uniquely different. You'll be exploring fun and outdoor activities. This retreat is intentionally kept small so guests can connect on a deeper level and is perfect for married couples of all ages.

If you'd like to come earlier or stay after this retreat, please let us know at loveyourspouse@retreat.com

Marriage Enrichment Topics

  • Love Language: The 5 Love Languages
  • Unity: Working Together As One
  • Protect: Setting Boundaries
  • Romantic Suprises: Get To Know Your Spouse, Again
  • Godly Marriage: Practical Tips
  • The Ultimate: Life Bigger Than Marriage
